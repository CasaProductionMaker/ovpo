OverPowered's Mission

Our mission at OverPowered HQ is to create a fun community where people play to have fun, not just for the sake of grinding. There are many ways we accomplish this, scroll down to find out more!

No Requirement

At OverPowered, we have no requirement for joining. This means even if you just started playing yesterday, you can join as long as you have a username! The reason for this is that we don't want to limit our guild to only the people with a Super, or level 100 because that defeats the purpose of our guild. We are trying to have fun, not to play for the sake of playing.

Events

We host many events in the OverPowered Discord server! We do a variety of events, from simple grinding ones, to PvP ones! This allows players to play with aspects of Florr that aren't normally used.

The Guild Utopia

OverPowered is part of a guild union called The Guild Utopia. The Guild Utopia is a Discord server consisting of many guilds, including OnEG, Hyhg, and more! In the server, we host many events and have channels dedicated to finding squadmates!
